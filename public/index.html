About These Books

These resources were written to give students and teachers a complete, high-quality set of materials for the Senior Short Course: Numeracy — at no cost.

📚

Student Workbook

A full 15-chapter workbook covering both Topic 1 (Personal Identity and Community) and Topic 2 (Workplace and Employment). Each chapter includes worked examples, practice exercises, application problems, and chapter reviews.

  • 15 chapters across 18 teaching weeks
  • Practice, Application & Review exercises
  • Multi-step problem sections
  • Two full assessment task instruments
  • QCAA Formula Reference Sheet included
📋

Teacher's Resource Book

A comprehensive companion for teachers, covering every chapter of the student workbook. Contains everything needed to plan and deliver the full 18-week course.

  • Teaching guides for every chapter
  • Fully worked solutions to every question
  • Common misconceptions & strategies
  • Marking guides for both assessment tasks
  • 72 individual lesson plans
  • Assessment Task 2 examination paper

Who Is This For?

🏫

Senior Students

Students enrolled in or preparing for the Senior Short Course: Numeracy in Queensland. Particularly suited to students who benefit from a structured, real-world approach to numeracy.

✍

Classroom Teachers

Teachers delivering the Senior Short Course: Numeracy who want a complete, print-ready resource set — without having to create materials from scratch. Includes everything from lesson plans to marking guides.

🏠

Home Educators

Parents and home educators supporting a student through Queensland senior numeracy. The workbook is self-contained and clearly structured for independent use.

📉

Tutors

Private tutors working with students on their Senior Numeracy Short Course. The teacher's book provides full worked solutions and teaching notes to make sessions more effective.
